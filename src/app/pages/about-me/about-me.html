About Me

I am a visual designer who loves building expressive interfaces and clear, human experiences. My work blends brand identity, UI systems, and playful web moments that still feel purposeful.

I started by exploring print and motion design, then moved toward interactive product work. Today I focus on crafting thoughtful UI with strong hierarchy, careful spacing, and details that make interfaces feel alive without getting in the way.

When I am not designing, I am experimenting with new tools, refining workflows, and learning how to make complex concepts feel simple and approachable.

This project is a personal playground that recreates a familiar operating system in the browser while showcasing my work, ideas, and the details I love to obsess over.
