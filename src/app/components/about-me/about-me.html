About Me

I am a software engineer with full-stack experience, specializing in building scalable, high-performance web applications and distributed systems. My primary focus spans front-end development using Angular and modern UI component libraries, as well as backend engineering with Go (Golang) and Spring Boot, where I design and implement robust APIs, microservices, and data-driven services.

In my professional work, I emphasize clean architecture, performance optimization, and reliability across the stack. I have hands-on experience developing RESTful services, handling concurrency and asynchronous workflows in Go, and building secure, maintainable backend systems using Spring Boot. I also work extensively with test automation and quality assurance, leveraging frameworks like Cypress to ensure stability in large-scale enterprise applications.

Alongside industry development, I bring a strong research and problem-solving mindset. I have worked on applied machine learning and deep learning projects, including computer vision, explainable AI, and multi-modal reasoning tasks such as Visual Commonsense Reasoning (VCR). My research interests lie in structured reasoning, graph-based transformers, and the integration of AI into real-world software systems.

I enjoy solving complex engineering challenges, whether optimizing frontend performance, designing scalable backend services, or translating research concepts into production- ready solutions. I value clarity, discipline, and continuous improvement, and I aim to build systems that are both technically sound and user-focused.

I am driven by long-term growth as an engineer and researcher, with a strong interest in building impactful products that combine thoughtful system design with intelligent automation.
